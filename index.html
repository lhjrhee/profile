I am a PhD student in Textile and Apparel Management at the University of Missouri. My research focuses on AI applications (e.g., large language models) in the fashion industry, and their downstream effects on fashion consumer behavior and retail analytics. My dissertation focuses on LLM-driven hyper-personalization and identity signaling among fashion consumers. Before my doctoral studies, I spent four years in the fashion and beauty industry in South Korea, as a merchandiser at CJ Olive Young, Sephora Korea, and Samsung C&T Fashion Group, which grounds my research in real-world retail practice.
